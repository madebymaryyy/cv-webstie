My work experience and projects

Security Management Console for Company Devices

In this project, I assisted in setting up a security management console (dashboard) that monitors and ensures the safety of all devices across the company. My role involved configuring the dashboard, creating detailed reports, and conducting network checks to verify the absence of infected devices. I also analyze user behavior to detect any suspicious activities. Currently, I handle security incidents and resolve any issues to maintain a secure environment.

NIS2 Compliance Application Development

I contributed to the development of an application aimed at helping businesses comply with the NIS2 directive. The app simplifies the process of determining whether a company falls under the NIS2 regulations. I focused on ensuring the application was user-friendly, accurate, and efficient, helping companies quickly assess their compliance status.

Security Audit for Pharmaceutical Applications

I conducted a comprehensive security audit of applications used by a major pharmaceutical company. My role involved reviewing the security status of various apps, identifying vulnerabilities, and recommending necessary improvements. Through this project, I helped strengthen the company's security posture by detecting and addressing critical issues in their software systems.

International Collaboration on NIST CSF vs NIS2

As part of an international project, I worked with a Japanese company to compare and analyze the American NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and the European NIS2 directive. I helped bridge the gap between the two frameworks, providing insights on how each can be applied in different regions. This project allowed me to collaborate on a global scale and deepen my understanding of international cybersecurity regulations.
